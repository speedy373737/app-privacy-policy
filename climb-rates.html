Privacy Policy

Climb Rates respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Information Accessed

The app uses the Wargaming Public API to retrieve publicly available player statistics from World of Warplanes. This data is already public and is not associated with any personally identifiable information.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by the app. The app does not track users, store profiles, or access any device data (such as contacts, camera, location, etc.).

Third Parties

The app does not transmit any data to third parties. All data is requested directly from the Wargaming API and is handled on the user's device.
